Jacob Black

Jacob Black is a character in the Twilight series by Stephenie Meyer. He is described as an attractive Native American of the Quileute tribe in La Push, near Forks, Washington. In the second book of the series(New Moon), he discovers that he is a therianthrope who can shapeshift into a wolf. For the majority of the series,Jacobs completes with Edward Cullen for Bella Swan's love. In the films Twilight,New Moon,Eclipse,Breaking Dawn: Part 1, and Breaking Dawn: Part 2,Jacob is played by Taylor Lautner.
